Commission Process

Check my Trello board for my commission queue. If the queue is full and I am currently not accepting new commissions, you may still contact me. However, I will not be able to start on your commission until a slot is available.

Once you've decided on a commission, please contact me with the details. I will provide you with a quote and a rough sketch. Once you've approved the sketch, I will start the final piece. You will receive a watermarked preview of the final piece. Once you've approved the final piece, you will receive the full resolution image.

Please have a visual reference of your character or idea ready. If you don't have one, please provide a detailed description.

My usual turnaround time is 1-2 weeks, but this may vary depending on the complexity of the commission and my current workload.

Payment & Money

  • Payment through PayPal.
  • All prices are in USD 🇺🇸, please pay me in USD, not your local currency.
  • Additional fees may be charged depending on the commission. The commission calculator on this website is just an approximation.
  • (Partial) payment is due upfront. I will start on your commission once payment is received.
  • Refunds are available if I have not started on your commission. Once I've started on your commission, refunds are not available.
